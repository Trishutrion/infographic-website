Consent Notice

Please read the information below carefully and thoroughly.

We would like to invite you to participate in a research survey conducted by us. The purpose of this survey is to find possible impacts of partaking in VCE on an individuals lifestyle.

Your participation in this survey is entirely voluntary, and you have the right to withdraw at any point without providing a reason. Before you decide whether to participate, it is important for you to understand the nature of the study and what will be expected of you as outlined below.

Procedure:

You will be asked to answer a range of questions about your VCE experience and how it has impacted your lifestyle.

Confidentiality:

Your responses will be kept confidential, and your identity will remain anonymous. No personally identifiable information will be disclosed or used in any publication or presentation resulting from this research.

Voluntary Involvement:

Your participation in this survey is entirely voluntary. If you choose not to participate or to withdraw from the survey, you will not face any negative consequences.

Contact Information:

If you have any questions or concerns about the survey, you may contact one of us via our emails.

By agreeing to participate in this survey, you confirm that you have read and understood the information provided in this consent form. You willingly agree to participate and provide your responses.

If you agree to participate, please click "Next".

Questions

    • What year level are you in?
      How many VCE Subjects are you currently doing?
      What raw study score do you think you will achieve for your VCE subjects?

      Approximately, what is your daily caloric intake?
      On average, how many hours do you sleep every day?
      On a scale of 0-10, how happy are you in general?
      On a scale of 0-10, how stressed are you in general?
      On a scale of 0-10, how satisfied are you in general?
      Lastly, are you optimistic about the future and what it holds for you?
