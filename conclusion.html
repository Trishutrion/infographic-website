Our Conclusions

by Arnav, Kyriakos, Ryan, and Branden

Our Findings

In conclusion, the impact of VCE subjects on the lifestyle of JMSS students is significant and multifaceted.
The data highlights a distinct preference for technical subjects such as Mathematics and Science, aligning with the school's specialization in these fields.
Despite lower average scores in Mathematics compared to Performing Arts, the higher scaling suggests greater academic challenge and reward.
This trend reflects JMSS students' optimistic anticipation of achieving higher study scores and their distinct attitude towards academic pursuits.
Moreover, the data underscores the school's emphasis on STEM fields, with a majority of students likely to pursue careers in related disciplines.
Overall, the correlation between VCE subject choices and student lifestyle at JMSS underscores a culture of academic rigor and ambition among its student body.
