How do VCE Subjects affect student lifestyle?

by Arnav, Kyriakos, Ryan, and Branden

About Us

As VCE students ourselves, we understand the challenges that come with the VCE program.
That's why we've chosen to explore the impact of VCE subject choices on student lifestyles.
Our mission is to provide valuable insights and information to help fellow students make informed decisions about their VCE journey.

The chosen topic for the infographic website is the impact of VCE subject choices on student lifestyles.
The purpose is to inform VCE students about the potential effects of their subject choices on various aspects of their lives.
The goal is to share insights and help students make informed decisions.
The target audience is teenage students in Victoria, as they are likely to enter the VCE program.
Primary data for the project comes from a survey that explores the influence of VCE subjects on sleep duration, physical activity, and average SAC scores.
Secondary data from the VCAA website will be used to compare the lifestyles of VCE and non-VCE students.
To maintain data integrity, measures have been taken to prevent multiple survey responses from the same individual.
Ethical data usage is prioritized by collecting anonymous data without personally identifiable information and ensuring that the
information is solely used for the website and not shared with third parties.
